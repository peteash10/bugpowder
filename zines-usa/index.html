The Zines

From August 2024 I’m focussing on scanning the covers and adding them to the spreadsheet - this should take a couple of months.

Thumbnails link to more legible larger versions. Metadata is pretty basic - many zines have more than one creator but I’ve just entered the main or first listed person for now, and “type” is very broad. This will all be expanded once the first pass is done. They’re sorted by creator and you can filter by type or country. Full search to come sometime in 2005.

750 covers from the 3-4,000 zines in the archive have been scanned so far with more added every few days.
