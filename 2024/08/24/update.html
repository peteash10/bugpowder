Update for 24 August 2024

Aug 24, 2024

New feature: The “items by the same creator” list at the bottom of individual item pages is now the same as the main zine listings, showing thumbnails and metadata. Have added a load of Colin Upton minis today which are a good example of this in action.

Added the following titles:

These are all minis (zines around or under A6 in size). Unlike the A4 magazine-sized zines, minis don’t really scan as well as I’d like, especially the tiny ones. They’re too three dimensional. I think a later project is going to be to photograph them as objects.
