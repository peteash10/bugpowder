Review zines are zines whose primary purpose is to list and offer appraisal of other zines. Most zines will have a page or two of reviews - this is how the network is sustained - but these zines will be all reviews, often acting as directories and gateways. This collection came about because I ran a review zine and can be seen as a record of what passed through one of the many nodes on the global network.
