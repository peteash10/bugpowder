The Zines

From August 2024 I’m focussing on scanning the covers and adding them to the spreadsheet - this should take a couple of months.

Thumbnails link to more legible larger versions. Metadata is pretty basic - many zines have more than one creator but I’ve just entered the main or first listed person for now, and “type” is very broad. This will all be expanded once the first pass is done. They’re sorted by creator and you can filter by type or country. Full search to come later.

839 zines from the aprox 4,000 in the archive have been processed with more added every few days.

Zines as a subcategory in this archive are generally text-based and tend to be about a subject or issue, engaging with it in a critical, conversational or exploratory way, often as part of a community or network of other zines. There is obviously an overlap with other categories which will contain publications that have zine-y aspects. (I am aware this is a bit confusing - everything in this archive is a zine but some zines are more “zine” than other zines!)
